WELCOME TO MONEYTHINK @ UCLA

Join us to transform lives by mentoring high school students in personal finance and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

Moneythink empowers under-resourced students to achieve college success by supporting financial decision-making through coaching and technology. Using the knowledge and resources we have, MoneyThink@UCLA interacts with high school students to provide a better knowledge of financial literacy. We connect with the students on a personal level and share the lessons we've learned throughout life.

Our Vision

We envision an equitable future in which all students have the opportunity to complete an affordable post-secondary education that puts them on a path to fulfillment and prosperity.

Financial literacy is something that is often overlooked throughout high schools, and our mission is to introduce these students to a world of finance that they may have never heard of.

What We Teach

Banking

An introduction to APR's, interest rates, and subsidized loans teaches students to make the best financial decision between different student loan options.

Retirement Planning

401k or long-term investments? We mentor students on the foundations to start planning for retirement from an early age.

Investment Strategy

From stocks and bonds to compounding interest and risk tolerance, our students are introduced to the world of investing.

About Us

Moneythink is a movement of young people restoring the economic health of the United States through financial education. Moneythink recruits, trains, and places UCLA students to serve as financial and career mentors for local high school and middle school students.
